WHY HWARANG

The Spirit of Hwarang

01

The Name of Hwarang

Hwarang (花郞) were the young leaders of Korea's ancient Silla Kingdom, admired for their courage, integrity, discipline and respect. Inspired by these timeless values, Hwarang Gogi brings the spirit of Korean hospitality and charcoal barbecue to every table.

02

Carefully Selected Quality

Every cut is carefully selected to deliver freshness, consistency and exceptional quality.

03

The Flavor of Charcoal Fire

Traditional Korean charcoal enhances the natural aroma, tenderness and rich flavor of every cut.

04

In the Heart of Hongdae

Just a five-minute walk from Hongik University Station Exit 9. Follow our video guide for the easiest way to find us.

FAQ

Before You Visit

Do I need a reservation?

No reservation is needed. Guests are seated in the order they arrive.

Is there a time limit?

Weekend evenings have a 100-minute time limit. There is no time limit on weekdays or weekend daytime.

What if there is a wait?

Waiting times vary depending on when you visit. Please check with our staff for current seating availability.

Can I pay with an international card?

Visa, Mastercard, and other internationally issued cards are accepted.

Can groups visit?

Please call ahead for group visits. Calling does not guarantee a table.
